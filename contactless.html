ContactlessTiara Salon has adopted a contactless experience method and specific measures to protect our guests and our staff.

Contactless ExperienceWe have been working hard behind the scenes to create the highest safety standards and, at the same time, continue to offer the Tiara Experience that you expect and love. The following forms are available to download for the contactless experience.

COVID-19 Pre-screening Form

This form is in place to help with contact tracing and is also recommended by the health officials. You will need to bring this form with you or fill one at the salon.

Contactless Payment Form

We will require a credit card on file before booking and arriving for an appointment. The card will not be charged until the day of the service.

When You Arrive

Please arrive early for your appointment and wait in your car. We will call you on your cell or wave you down when we are ready for you. Door will remain locked to reduce foot traffic.

Tiara CurbsideWe are splitting our group into shifts, thereby reducing the number of people in the salon at one time. To schedule product curbside pickup, please make arrangements at least 24 hours before the arrival. We greatly appreciate the cooperation in the efforts to create a safe environment.

Curbside

Click to call us when you are here. With Tiara Curbside, stay safely in your car and we'll bring your favorites out to you.
